Content

Home page

Introduction Welcome to my resume where as you read my resume website you can get to know me better and see if I am a fit for your company. There are many items on this page that you may find useful to learn more about me and how valuable I can be as an employee. I know that there are many needs and positions that I can capably fill and be of benefit to you and your business. If you have any questions please reach out to me through the contact information located at the bottom of this website. I look forward to hearing from you!

Accomplishments I have a variety of experiences working throughout the field of technology. Some accomplishments that I have made throughout this field are a Bachelor's degree in CIT (Computer Information Technology) and multiple certificates in cybersecurity. Additionally, I have worked on reports in cybersecurity, and have worked on many teams including the cybersecurity club at Brigham Young University-Idaho. In total, I have learned five programming languages which include: Python, C++, Hyper Text Markup Language (HTML), Cascading Style Sheets (CSS), Javascript, and Structured Query Langauge (SQL).

Experience I have experience working with a variety of computer programs. A few different types of programs that I have worked on are front and back-end development, machine learning, and database development. I have worked with the back end of computers extensively which includes networking and server-side programs. Python and SQL are the two languages I have the most experience with. I have been working with Python for five years and SQL for three years. I have configured my own network through a router that included a firewall. Additionally, I have experience participating in a cybersecurity activity called Capture the Flag. This activity gave me experience with finding vulnerabilities in computer systems.

Projects Over the years I have had the opportunity to participate in and lead a variety of technology-based projects. A few projects that I have participated in and completed are text-generation machine learning, roadsign recognition, RSA encryption server, and information databases. The projects that I used the language Python in are the text-generation machine learning, roadsign recognition, and RSA encryption server projects. The projects that I used the language SQL in were a variety of information databases.

Characteristics Throughout my experiences and learning I have grown as an individual, a teammate, and a learner. I am hardworking, trustworthy, and reliable. Working in groups has shown me that I can be a reliable teammate. This was shown as I completed my portion of assignments in a timely manner and before the due date. I have also helped my teammates and classmates understand the content of an assignment or lesson when needed. I am also organized and flexible. While working in groups and partnerships I have learned to be open to others ideas and adaptive to the execution of ideas by others. Being flexible has allowed me to have a rapport with my teammates and also be exposed to a variety of ideas and concepts. I had the opportunity to serve a two-year mission on behalf of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. While serving I was able to develop and grow in my communication and social skills. Throughout those two years, I talked to new people every day and taught them new concepts or ideas about my religion. As a result, I can clearly communicate ideas to others, express my opinion, and teach others in a way that they can understand. Overall, these skills that I possess allow me to be an effective employee and coworker.
